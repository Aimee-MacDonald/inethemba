Inethemba Management is a Bulawayo based arts management and consulting company. We are comprised of three divisions: music and arts production, events coordination, and artist management. Each focusing on a different exciting aspect of the arts and cultural industry.

Inethemba Management grew from a passion to engaging with the arts and culture through solid business practices and opertions. With a deep understanding of both the arts industry and the nature of management and business, founders Desmond Ntini and Austin Dube have created a strong, competitive company in the arts and cultural industry in Bulawayo.

Inethemba Management works with performing arts organisations and musicians in all stages of development - from emerging artists and organisations to established performers, directors, producers, fundraisers, and marketing specialists. Through its services, Inethemba Management empowers arts and culture practitioners to create and sustain a thriving industry so that communities can be enlivened by the arts.

Working with the most notable artists in the bulawayo modern music scene, Inethemba Management has signed and works with acts such as Qeqe, Thandie Dlana, De Lukes & Band, The Sets, Thulani Harsh Touch and DJ Pidin.

Inethemba Management works with three theatre companies to assist with the development of theatre productions. This project has helped the theatre companies develop inspiring and engaging productions like Gukurahundi, The Hostel, and Double Funeral.

As part of their creative endeavour, Inethemba Management is the producer of the upcoming musical theatre production Nyaminyami.

Inethemba Management has played a leading role in the strategic planning of events like Intwasa, Ibumba, Bulawayo Arts Awards and many others.
